About Us

At Snap Check, we're dedicated to revolutionizing the way people detect allergens in their food. Our innovative OCR-based technology simplifies the process of identifying potential allergens, making it easier for individuals to make informed choices about what they eat. With Snap Check, users can simply scan the label of a food product using their smartphone, and our advanced OCR engine will quickly and accurately extract the ingredient list, detecting potential allergens in real-time.

Features:

  • Fast and Accurate Scanning:

    Snap Check's OCR technology quickly extracts ingredient lists from food labels, providing users with accurate and reliable information.

  • Real-time Allergen Detection:

    Our system detects potential allergens in real-time, empowering users to make informed decisions about their food choices.

  • User-Friendly Interface:

    Snap Check's intuitive interface makes it easy for users to scan labels, view results, and manage their allergen profiles.
